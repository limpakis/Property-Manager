# 7-DAY MVP LAUNCH PLAN
## Property Management Business

**Target Launch Date:** February 13, 2026

---

## DAY 1 (Feb 6) - LEGAL FOUNDATION
**Critical Path - Cannot skip**

### Morning (3 hours)
- [ ] Register LLC online (30 min)
  - LegalZoom, ZenBusiness, or state website
  - Cost: $100-300
- [ ] Apply for EIN (IRS website, free, instant) (15 min)
- [ ] Open business bank account (1 hour)
  - Bring LLC docs + EIN confirmation
  - Chase, Bank of America, or local credit union
- [ ] Open separate trust/escrow account (1 hour)
  - Required for holding tenant deposits
  - Same bank for simplicity

### Afternoon (3 hours)
- [ ] Check state property management license requirement (30 min)
  - Google: "[Your State] property management license requirements"
  - If required: Start application process
  - If not required: Document exemption
- [ ] Get E&O insurance quote (1 hour)
  - Call 3 insurance brokers
  - Coverage: $1M minimum
  - Cost estimate: $500-1500/year
- [ ] Find real estate attorney (1 hour)
  - Google: "[Your City] real estate attorney"
  - Call 5 offices, get quotes for lease review
  - Schedule appointment for Day 2

### Evening (1 hour)
- [ ] Customize all documents with your details
  - Company name, phone, email, address
  - Update: management agreement, lease, marketing letter
- [ ] Set up business phone number
  - Google Voice (free) or dedicated line

**END OF DAY 1 CHECKLIST:**
✓ LLC registered  
✓ EIN obtained  
✓ Bank accounts opened  
✓ Attorney appointment scheduled  
✓ Documents customized

## 15. TERMINATION
- Either party may terminate month-to-month lease with 30-day written notice
- Tenant breaking fixed-term lease is liable for rent through lease end
- Landlord may terminate for lease violations with proper notice per state law

## 16. DEFAULT AND EVICTION
Tenant is in default if:
- Rent unpaid by 10th of month
- Lease violations not cured within notice period
- Property abandoned
- Illegal activity occurs

Landlord may pursue eviction and damages per state law.

## 17. HOLDOVER
If Tenant remains after lease end without agreement, holdover rent is [150%] of monthly rent, prorated daily.

## 18. ATTORNEY FEES
Prevailing party in any legal dispute recovers reasonable attorney fees.

- Clean property thoroughly
- Repair any damages
- Remove all personal property
- Forward mailing address for deposit return

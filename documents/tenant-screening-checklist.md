- [ ] Current address (2+ years history)
- [ ] Current employer and income
- [ ] Emergency contact
- [ ] Vehicle information
- [ ] Pet information
- [ ] Number of occupants
- [ ] Application fee collected ($50)

## Credit Check
- [ ] Credit score: _____ (minimum 600)
- [ ] Credit report pulled via [MySmartMove/TransUnion/Experian]
- [ ] Review for:
  - [ ] Collections or charge-offs
  - [ ] High debt-to-income ratio
  - [ ] Recent missed payments
  - [ ] Bankruptcies
  - [ ] Foreclosures

## Background Check
- [ ] Criminal history check completed
- [ ] Review for:

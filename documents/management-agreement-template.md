# PROPERTY MANAGEMENT AGREEMENT

**Date:** [DATE]  
**Property Owner:** [OWNER NAME]  
**Property Address:** [FULL ADDRESS]  
**Manager:** [YOUR COMPANY NAME]

## 1. APPOINTMENT OF MANAGER
Owner appoints Manager as exclusive agent to manage the Property.

## 2. TERM
This agreement begins on [START DATE] and continues month-to-month. Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.

## 3. MANAGEMENT FEE
- **Monthly Fee:** 10% of collected rent
- **Lease Placement Fee:** One month's rent for new tenant placement
- **Renewal Fee:** $200 per lease renewal
- **Minimum Monthly Fee:** $100 (applies even if property vacant)

## 4. MANAGER RESPONSIBILITIES
- Market and show property to prospective tenants
- Screen tenants (credit, background, employment verification)
- Collect rent and security deposits
- Coordinate maintenance and repairs
- Conduct property inspections (move-in, quarterly, move-out)
- Handle tenant communications and complaints
- Enforce lease terms
- Process evictions if necessary
- Provide monthly financial statements

## 5. OWNER RESPONSIBILITIES
- Maintain property insurance
- Pay property taxes and HOA fees
- Approve repairs over $500
- Maintain property to habitable standards
- Fund operating account for repairs

## 6. SECURITY DEPOSITS
All security deposits held in Manager's trust account. Returned to tenant per state law, minus deductions for damages.

## 7. MAINTENANCE AUTHORIZATION
Manager authorized to spend up to $500 per incident for emergency repairs without prior approval. Non-emergency repairs over $500 require Owner approval.

## 8. ACCOUNTING
Manager provides monthly statement by 10th of following month. Owner funds distributed within 5 business days of rent collection.

## 9. TERMINATION
Upon termination, Manager provides all keys, documents, and tenant files. Final accounting provided within 15 days.

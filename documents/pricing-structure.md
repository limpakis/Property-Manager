# PRICING STRUCTURE

## Standard Fees

### Monthly Management
- **10%** of collected rent
- **Minimum:** $100/month (even if vacant)

### Tenant Placement
- **One month's rent** for new tenant placement
- Includes: marketing, showings, screening, lease preparation, move-in inspection

### Lease Renewal
- **$200** per renewal
- Includes: market analysis, renewal negotiation, updated lease

### Maintenance Markup
- **15%** markup on all vendor services
- Covers coordination, quality control, documentation

### Additional Services
- Eviction processing: $500 + court costs
- Property inspections (owner-requested): $75
- Tenant move-out coordination: Included
- After-hours emergency coordination: Included
- Financial reporting: Included

## Introductory Offer (First 10 Clients)
- **6%** monthly management fee (first 6 months)
- **50%** off tenant placement ($0.5 month rent)
- All other fees standard

## Payment Terms
- Management fees deducted from collected rent before owner distribution
- Placement fees due at lease signing
- Additional service fees invoiced separately, due within 15 days

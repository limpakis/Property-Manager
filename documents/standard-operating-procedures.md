# STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES

## New Property Onboarding

1. **Initial Meeting**
   - Property walkthrough
   - Document current condition with photos (minimum 30 photos)
   - Note existing issues/deferred maintenance
   - Obtain keys, gate codes, HOA info

2. **Documentation Collection**
   - Copy of deed or proof of ownership
   - Existing leases (if occupied)
   - Warranty information for appliances
   - HOA rules and contact info
   - Utility company information
   - Vendor contacts owner currently uses

3. **Account Setup**
   - Add property to tracking system
   - Create property file (digital and physical)
   - Set up owner in accounting system
   - Add to inspection schedule

4. **Marketing Preparation** (if vacant)
   - Professional photos
   - Competitive rent analysis
   - List on Zillow, Apartments.com, Craigslist
   - Install lockbox for showings

## Tenant Screening Process

**Minimum Requirements:**
- Credit score 600+
- Income 3x monthly rent
- No evictions in past 7 years
- No violent criminal history
- Positive landlord references

**Process:**
1. Application submitted with $50 fee
2. Credit and background check (use MySmartMove or similar)
3. Employment verification (pay stubs + call employer)
4. Previous landlord reference (minimum 2)
5. Present findings to owner with recommendation
6. Owner approves/denies within 24 hours

## Maintenance Request Handling

**Emergency (respond within 1 hour):**
- No heat/AC (extreme temperatures)
- Plumbing leak causing damage
- Electrical hazard
- Lock-out (after verifying tenancy)
- Gas leak

**Urgent (respond within 24 hours):**
- Non-functioning appliance
- HVAC failure (moderate temps)
- Pest infestation
- Non-emergency plumbing

**Standard (respond within 72 hours):**
- Minor repairs
- Cosmetic issues
- Preventive maintenance

**Process:**
1. Tenant submits request via phone/email
2. Log in maintenance tracking system
3. Assess urgency level
4. Dispatch appropriate vendor
5. Get owner approval if >$500 (emergency exception)
6. Track completion
7. Follow up with tenant
8. Document with photos
9. Process vendor invoice

## Rent Collection

**Timeline:**
- Rent due: 1st of month
- Grace period: Through 5th
- Late fee applied: 6th ($50 + $10/day)
- 3-day pay or quit notice: 10th
- Eviction filing: 15th (if unpaid)

**Process:**
1. Automated reminders sent: 28th, 1st, 3rd
2. Manual follow-up if unpaid by 6th
3. Apply late fees per lease
4. Issue pay or quit notice if unpaid by 10th
5. Contact owner about eviction if unpaid by 15th

## Property Inspections

**Move-In:**
- Complete within 48 hours of tenant move-in
- Document every room with photos
- Tenant signs inspection report
- Copy to tenant and file

**Quarterly:**
- Schedule with tenant (minimum 48-hour notice)
- 20+ photos per visit
- Check smoke detectors, filters, pest issues
- Note any lease violations or needed repairs
- Report to owner within 24 hours

**Move-Out:**
- Schedule for lease end date or within 24 hours of vacancy
- Document condition thoroughly (50+ photos)
- Compare to move-in inspection
- Prepare itemized deduction list within 48 hours
- Return security deposit per state timeline

## Owner Communication

**Monthly:**
- Financial statement by 10th of following month
- Property photos with quarterly inspection
- Maintenance summary

**As Needed:**
- Approval requests for repairs >$500 (within 4 hours of issue)
- Tenant issues or lease violations (within 24 hours)
- Emergency repairs (immediate notification)

## Financial Procedures

**Trust Account:**
- All tenant deposits and rent collected in trust account
- Never commingle with business funds
- Monthly reconciliation

**Owner Distributions:**
- Calculate by 5th of month: Collected rent - management fee - expenses
- Transfer to owner account by 8th of month
- Statement uploaded to owner portal

**Expense Tracking:**
- All receipts photographed and filed
- Categorized by property
- Reconciled monthly
